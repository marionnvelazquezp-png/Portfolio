Vaitiare Spa & Skin

2018 — Present

Vaitiare started as a small beauty clinic with a great product and almost no digital presence. I built their entire marketing foundation from scratch — identifying laser hair removal as their star service when few competitors offered it in the city, and making it the centerpiece of every campaign.

The strategy was organic-first: social SEO, local events, strategic influencer partnerships, and brand activations — all before spending on paid ads. Once their metrics were solid, we introduced quarterly ad budgets that eventually took them to television. I also led a full rebrand, evolving their visual identity from the original green neon logo to a refined, spa-grade aesthetic that reflects where the brand is today.

Vaitiare now operates two full-service clinics in Puebla with city-wide top-of-mind recognition — a direct result of sustained brand building and smart channel mix management.

Personal Brand · Lifestyle & Influencer

Maruza Aparicio

2018 — Present

When Maruza came to me she had 1,200 followers and a genuine personality but no clear brand strategy. My goal was to turn her into a recognizable figure whose name alone opens doors — for her businesses, collaborations, and ventures.

I designed her full content system: editorial formats, posting cadence, story strategy, and visual identity. I handled all video and photo editing so she could focus solely on showing up. The strategy was almost entirely organic — paid media only for specific business campaigns, never for personal brand growth.

Today she's a verified creator with 511K+ followers across platforms, 70+ brand collaborations including national and international names, and four business ventures under her name (@casamaruza, @glowpassmx, @maruza.acosmetics, @casamaruza.bazar) — proof that a strong personal brand compounds into real business value.
